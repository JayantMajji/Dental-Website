ARE YOU FACING ANY DENTAL ISSUES?

Previous Next

Tooth Decay


Preventions


-Avoid sweets & chocolates before going to bed
-Avoid in between meal snacking
-Brush twice a day
-Regular visits to you dentist recommended

Tooth Erosion


Preventions


-Avoid sleeping as soon as eating meals if gastric problems present
-Pregnant women recommended to take less acidic food
-Visit gastroenterologist
-Visit dentist for further treatment

Tooth Sensitivity


Preventions


-Avoid scrub type of brushing
-Avoid habits like clenching teeth & bruxism
-Consult dentist for above habit breaking
-Use toothpastes for sensitivity (Vantej)

Oral Cancer


Preventions


-Avoid cigarette & tobacco smoking
-Avoid kaini, ghutka, zarda, tobacco chewing
-Visit rehabilitation centre to quit the above habits
-Periodic visits to doctor recommended

Mouth Sores


Preventions


-Avoid unnecessary stresses
-Avoid cheek biting habit
-Vitamin supplements recommended (B-complex)
-Stress reduction protocol should be taken

Periodontal Issues


Preventions


-Follow right tooth brushing technique
-Gargle mouth after every meal
-Visit your dentist regularly
-Get your teeth cleaned every six months

Orthodontics


Preventions


-Avoid early extraction of primary/deciduous teeth if possible
-Avoid habits like thumb sucking, lip biting, tongue thrusting
-Habit breaking appliances for children
-Space maintainers in case of early extraction of primary tooth

Tooth aches


Preventions


-Avoid sweets & chocolates before going to bed
-Avoid horizontal tooth brushing method
Early consultation to dentist when decay noticed
Visit physician for any underlying malignancies

Unattractive Smile


Preventions


-Avoid habits like tongue thrusting & lip biting
-Avoid extraction of decayed teeth if possible
-Prosthetic surgeries suggested
-Visit dentist for prosthesis of missing teeth & consult orthodontist for above habit breaking
